## Artiss

## Location

Behind OR 3 -> Left Minifridge

## Details

Used as a biologic adhesive in burn cases. Comes in 3 standard sizes:

- 2mL
- 4mL
- 10mL

## Notes

Will require thawing prior to use. Please get these out to thaw prior to needing them. If required, can use warm saline to thaw. Once thawed, has a 2 week viability window. Please mark the date thawed on the box if not used.
Requires Artiss tubing (found in the burn cabinet, high on the right).
